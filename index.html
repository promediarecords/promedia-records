Zimora El New

Zimora El is a dynamic Afro-fusion and spiritual music artist known for blending Afrobeat, Amapiano, and English-Jamaican vocal textures into a bold, uplifting sound. Her music carries themes of faith, identity, resilience, and divine fire, often mixing melodic storytelling with high-energy performance. With a voice that can move from soulful worship to hype-driven chants, Zimora El creates songs that feel cinematic, prophetic, and vibrant. Her artistry is marked by expressive delivery, spiritual depth, and a unique fusion of contemporary African rhythms with Caribbean-inspired flavor.

Signature Style
Afro-fusion • Spiritual Afrobeat • English-Jamaican inflection • High-energy praise sound
Known For
Powerful choruses, prophetic lyrics, bold vocal attitude, and “holy fire” energy.
